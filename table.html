Welcome to convocation ceremony

Introduction

Welcome to the official webpage for EASTC'S Convocation ceremony 2025. This significant event takes place the day before graduation each year, serving as a special occasion to celebrate the achievements of our graduates.

Overview of the 7th Convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre (EASTC)

The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Tanzania
The 7th convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre of the year 2024 continues the tradition within academic institutions of gathering together academicians, students, alumni, benefactors, and industry professionals as stakeholders of the university community. It is the time to exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching, research, and professional development.

This year's convocation has selected the topic: "The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Tanzania", as the standing point to reflect on the theme of this year's 18th of November African statistics Day. The theme states:
"Modernizing data ecosystems to accelerate the implementation of African continental free trade Area (AICFTA): The role statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Africa"
